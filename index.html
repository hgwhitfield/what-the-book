Welcome to What The Book! Here's how you can make the most of our site:

  • Search for Books: Use the search bar to find books by title, author name, or ISBN. Simply type in your query and hit Enter or click the search icon.
  • View Search Results: Browse through the search results to find books that interest you. Each result includes a book cover, title, and author.
  • Book Details: Click on a book cover or title to open a new tab with more details about the book on Google Books.
  • Add to To Be Read (TBR) List: Click the "Add to TBR" button on any book to save it to your personal reading list. Your TBR list is stored locally in your browser.
  • View Your TBR List: Click the "To Be Read List" button to see all the books you've added. You can also click on any book in this list to view more details on Google Books.
  • Remove Books from TBR List: If you want to remove a book from your TBR list, simply click the "Remove" button under the book in your TBR list.

Enjoy discovering and organizing your next great reads with Book Finder!
